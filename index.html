About me

Hi, I'm Thea. A registered nurse transitioning into the tech field. After several years in healthcare, I discovered how much I enjoy creating intuitive, visual and user-friendly digital experiences, and I decided to explore that passion further. I'm currently studying Frontend Development at Noroff, building projects that help me grow one line of code at a time. I enjoy working from idea to finished product, starting with wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and turning them into functional websites using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I'm highly organized, love learning new things and enjoy refining both my design and development workflows. My background in healthcare has shaped me in ways that benefit my work in tech, I'm calm under pressure, detail-oriented and I genuinly care about creating solutions that make sense for real people. I'm currently looking for opportunities to grow as a developer and contribute to meaningful projects. When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me knitting or reading.
